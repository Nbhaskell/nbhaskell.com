A little about myself

I hail from the great white north.. Maine to be specific.
Short days and long cold winters didn't forward me as much "outdoorsy"
time as other places, so I spent my time exploring my artistic abilities
through various mediums and tinkering with anything tech related. I knew
early on that I would do one, or a combination of the two professionally.
So that is exactly what I did. I am a designer, full stack web developer, and
software engineer by trait. Now I live in beautiful sunny San Diego, California
with my stunning wife Ryann, who is a San Diego native (woot woot!).

I am currently available for freelance work so if you have a website or project in mind,
